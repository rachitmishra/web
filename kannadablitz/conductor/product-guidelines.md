# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Voice
- **Balanced and Encouraging:** The application should feel like a supportive coach. Use lighthearted language and emojis to celebrate progress, but remain concise and direct during instruction to respect the user's time.
- **Structured Learning:** While the delivery is playful, the presentation of Kannada phrases and rules should be authoritative and well-organized, ensuring users feel confident in the accuracy of the material.

## Visual Identity
- **Modern and Vibrant:** Use a clean, contemporary aesthetic with a palette that feels energetic (e.g., using "Blitz" as a theme for speed and energy).
- **Gamified Elements:** Visual cues like hearts, streaks, and badges should be prominent and satisfying to interact with.
- **Accessibility First:** Ensure high contrast for text and clear typography (Inter/Bowlby One as currently used) to make learning comfortable on all devices.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Frictionless Navigation:** The transition between dashboard, flashcards, and scenarios should be seamless.
- **Immediate Feedback:** Provide instant visual and audio feedback for correct and incorrect answers in scenarios.
- **Habit-Centric Design:** Focus on "low barrier to entry" daily tasks to encourage consistency.
