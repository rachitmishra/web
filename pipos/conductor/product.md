# Product Guide - Mellopop

## Initial Concept
Mellopop is a gamified piano learning and practice application designed to make musical education accessible and engaging for beginners of all ages.

## Target Users
- **Children:** Learning piano for the first time through play and interaction.
- **Adult Beginners:** Looking for an approachable, gamified way to start their musical journey.

## Primary Goals
- **Engagement:** Create a fun, motivating environment that encourages consistent daily practice.
- **Progress Tracking:** Provide clear visualizations of skill development and achievements.
- **Accessibility:** Break down the barriers to piano learning for those without traditional formal instruction.

## Key Features
- **Interactive Piano Interface:** A responsive virtual keyboard supporting multiple input methods.
- **Gamified Lessons:** A structured progression of challenges and levels that reward user improvement.
- **Social Features:** Community engagement through leaderboards and progress sharing.
- **Guided Tutorials:** Step-by-step song lessons and technique walkthroughs.
- **Sheet Music Analysis:** Tools to integrate and interact with digital or scanned sheet music.
- **Progressive Difficulty:** Intelligent adjustment of challenge levels based on real-time performance.
